Founder

Shishir Shrivastava

Founder & Sponsor — JBV REAM

Prior to founding JBV, Shishir Shrivastava served as Group CEO & Managing Director of The Phoenix Mills Limited, where over a 24-year tenure he transformed the company from a single Mumbai asset into India's leading retail-led mixed-use real estate platform — a pan-India portfolio spanning over 20 million square feet across retail, hospitality, commercial, and residential sectors.

His tenure was defined by the structuring of large-scale joint ventures with global institutional capital, and by building the governance and operating infrastructure required to meet institutional LP standards. An alumnus of IIM Calcutta and the Harvard Business School Advanced Management Program, Shishir brings together deep operational expertise with a sophisticated command of capital markets and investment strategy.

· ₹58,000 Cr. market cap scaled under his leadership

Co-Founder

Rahul Nair

Co-Founder & Investment Management Lead — JBV REAM

Rahul brings over two decades of experience across real estate investment, hospitality M&A, and institutional capital markets. His career spans leadership roles at Everstone Group — across Evercloud, Indospace, and Everstone Capital — and the India Hotels Company (Taj Hotels), where he led M&A transactions. Earlier in his career, he held positions at ING Barings and Bank of America.

His approach to investment management is grounded in structural integrity, disciplined underwriting, and long-term value preservation. At JBV, Rahul leads the investment management function, overseeing deal origination, structuring, and capital allocation across the platform.
