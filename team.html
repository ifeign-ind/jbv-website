Founder

Shishir Shrivastava

Founder & Sponsor — JBV REAM

Prior to founding JBV, Shishir Shrivastava was the Managing Director at The Phoenix Mills Limited (PML). Over his 24-year tenure, he played a pivotal role in transforming PML into India's leading retail-led mixed-use real estate developer. Under his leadership, the company expanded its footprint from a single asset in Mumbai to a pan-India portfolio comprising over 20 million square feet across retail, hospitality, commercial, and residential sectors.

His strategic vision was instrumental in forging high-profile joint ventures with global institutional investors, attracting over $1 billion in institutional capital. An alumnus of IIM Calcutta and the Harvard Business School Advanced Management Program, Shishir combines deep operational expertise with a sophisticated understanding of capital markets and investment strategy.

· Group CEO & MD — The Phoenix Mills Ltd. (2015–2025)
· ₹58,000 Cr. market cap scaled under his leadership

Co-Founder

Rahul Nair

Co-Founder & Investment Management Lead — JBV REAM

Rahul brings over two decades of experience across real estate investment, hospitality M&A, and institutional capital markets. His career spans leadership roles at Everstone Group — across Evercloud, Indospace, and Everstone Capital — and the India Hotels Company (Taj Hotels), where he led M&A transactions. Earlier in his career, he held positions at ING Barings and Bank of America.

His approach to investment management is grounded in structural integrity, disciplined underwriting, and long-term value preservation. At JBV, Rahul leads the investment management function, overseeing deal origination, structuring, and capital allocation across the platform.
